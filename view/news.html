Annual Camp 2020/21

Published on : 07 NOV 2021

A compass marching activity was conducted on 29 January to 31 January for 3 days and 2 nights. This compass marching activity was conducted at the Ulu Choh Dam and ended at the Gunung Pulai Amusement Forest. A total of 42 Junior Cadet Officers participated in this compass marching activity. Of the total, 4 syndicates were divided and each syndicate was provided with a topographic map of Kulai-Skudai district along with a compass.

The purpose of this activity is to test the physical and mental endurance of each Junior Cadet Officer when in the forest. In addition, this activity was also conducted to test the understanding of each Junior Cadet Officer on learning to read maps and skills in using a compass. Before starting this activity, each Officer Cadet was given a description of the role of each individual so that unwanted things can be avoided.

KSMJ Handover Ceremony

Published on : 30 JUNE 2021

The Handover Ceremony of the Head Coach was held at the PALAPES UMP Gambang Headquarters on 02 January. The ceremony was attended by Major Mohd Faudzi bin Zahari, Lieutenant Mohd Norhisham bin Ibrahim as the Head Coach and Acting Head Coach of UMP Land PALAPES. Staff and coaches as well as cadets from senior, intermediate and junior levels were also present to enliven the event.
